Long, long ago there lived a priest, who had a little baby.
Amazingly, his baby was a snake.
He ordered the snake to meditate in a mountain. One day, the people of the village went hunting for a wild animal,
for a village feast. They found a big snake and cut
it into pieces. They didn't know that it
was the priest's child.
The spirit of the snake became a small boy.
He begged for food when the villagers were having feast.
But no one cared for the boy.
The little boy then planted a stick.
When the stick was pulled out of the ground,
water sprang out of the hole.
Gradually it became a big flood.
All the villagers, except an old widow, were drowned.
She was saved because she had helped the little
boy by giving him some food and drink.
